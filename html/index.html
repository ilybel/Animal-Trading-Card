The Leatherback Sea Turtle

The origin of sea turtles goes back to the Late Jurrasic (150 million years ago)

  • Scientific Name: Chelonioidea
  • Average Length: 6-9 ft
  • Lifespan: About 80 years
  • Habitat: Oceans

The leatherback is the largest species of sea turtle and, can weigh up to 700 kilograms. Their primary prey are jellyfish and other gelatinous plankton, whose fluids have the same concentration of salts as sea water.
